Who I am:

My name is Lance Porter and I am a Visual Designer and Front-end developer born & raised in New Jersey, but currently contracting in Michigan.

I underwent classical design training at Eastern Michigan University, where I received a Bachelors of Fine Arts with a concentration in Graphic Design and Visual Communication. I have extensive experience creating multimedia assets in Adobe Creative Suite and its competing alternatives (Sketch, Paintshop Pro).

I am fluent in HTML5, CSS3, Sass/Less pre-processors, CLI utilities, and I’m always increasing my Javascript literacy. I have experience with open source CMS software like Wordpress and Drupal and am currently experimenting with MVC frameworks like Angular.

I am currently in the exploratory phases of learning back-end technologies like PHP, Python, Ruby, Node.js and Go on a deeper level.
